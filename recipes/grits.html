Grits

Grits are a southern staple in the United States. They can be paired with a variety of foods or by themselves. Grits are a quick and easy food.

Ingredients

  • Grits
  • Butter
  • Water
  • Cheese (I prefer sharp cheddar)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Put 1 1/2 cups of water in a pot to boil.
  2. Once boiling add 1/4 cups of grits to water.
  3. Turn heat down to simmer.
  4. Add salt while grits simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Stir occasionally.
  6. After 5 minutes are up add a 1/2 cup of cheese.
  7. Stir until melted.
  8. Remove from heat and serve.
